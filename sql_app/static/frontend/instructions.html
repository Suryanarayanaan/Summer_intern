Face Recognition Instructions

  • Ensure that the webcam is open and functional.
  • Do not wear a mask during the face recognition process.
  • Ensure that the lighting conditions are appropriate for accurate results.
  • Keep your face steady and avoid any movement or shaking.
  • Multiple individuals should not be present in the camera frame simultaneously.
  • Maintain a safe distance from the camera.
  • Photos will be captured for the purpose of face recognition.
  • If the results are not satisfactory, please retry the process.
  • Please be patient as the recognition process may take up to a minute.
